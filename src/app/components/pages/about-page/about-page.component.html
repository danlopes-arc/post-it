About

Here you can follow other users and see their posts on your timeline.
by Daniel Lopes Arcanjo, #8679485
  • Recommended: seed the database first on the settings page
  • The explore page shows you the users you are not following yet.
  • The following page shows people you are... well, following.
  • The timeline shows posts from you and from people you are following.
  • Tap new post to create a post.
  • You can edit or delete your post, not other's.
  • When creating or editing a post you can save your location and make it public to the other users.
  • The post also shows comments, and lets you create those as well.
  • You can edit and delete your comments, not other's.
  • Tapping on an user's username takes you to their profile page. Tapping on your username up top takes you to yours.
  • The profile page show the user's posts and an option to follow/unfollow them. Yours let you edit it and logout.
  • The settings page let's you clear and/or seed the database. Seeding is highly recommended.
